About the hobby (for the noobs!)

Common items used

  • Paints- There are many brands of paint when it comes to plastic models, Citadel and Vallejo are the most popular.
  • Models- You can get all sorts of models from small dogs to giant robots, you can also get houses and other scenery.
  • Tools- There are so many tools that can make the hobby easier; files, pin drills and other types of brushes are a few examples.
  • Inspiration- There are loads of poeple on platforms such as YouTube, who make content based on the hobby, this can help give you new ideas.

hopefully you'll make friends and learn something new and fun!
